FEATUR FILM

CHRONICLE OF REPRESSION

Chronical of Repression is a psychological thriller about Rafi, a pharmaceutical company scientist, who is urgently called to pick his wife from the hospital after suffering a violent rape. When they come home, she locks herself in the bathroom and Rafi steps out for a breath of air. Upon his return, he discovers that his wife disappeared and he sets out looking for her. During his question, Rafi discovers sides that he had not known about his wife and realizes that, in order to find her, he will first have to solve the mystery that she was obsessed about.

89 minutes

BIO

David Yakin is a graduate of the “Camera Obscura” school of film. Since graduating in 2012, he produced and directed three documentaries and one feature film. David honed his field production and photography skills while shooting dozens of magazine pieces and two documentary films for the international i24news channel. He also shot a documentary named “A Certificate of Identity”, which won first place at the 2012 DocAviv Film Festival. David also took part in two WZO projects and conducted over 150 interviews with soldiers of Israeli underground organizations, the War of Independence and 6-Day War. In addition, David served as the Head of Marketing Media at Sforweel Ltd, where he produced and directed promotional films.
